M E M B E R S H I P

Communications

Membership with PASA includes leader-to-leader communication that looks to stregnthen the connection between schools and communitites.

Advocacy

PASA advocates for its members in the areas of compensation and benefits, resources, professional development, and legislation.

Professional Development

Members recieve access to various trainings and learning opportunities throughout the year to enhance and extend practice and leadership capabilities.

Career Center

Stay connected to opportunities for career growth and development. Explore ways to further your career while supporting the mission of Pinellas County Schools.

Networking Opportunities

Participate in workshops, seminars, luncheons, and events to expand your professional network of school leaders and further your career.

Financial Assistance

Scholarship awards are given to members for various professional growth and development opportunities. Also, PASA has collegiate assistance scholarships for members children.
