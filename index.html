PINELLAS ASSOCIATION OF SCHOOLS ADMINISTRATION

Membership

From professional development to our mentoring and advocacy program, membership in the Pinellas Association of School Administrators is a great way to stay connected as an administrator.

Awards

PASA proudly promotes and sponsors many recognition programs for our members throughout the year, including professional development scholarship opportunities for members and collegiate assistance scholarships for members children.

Advocacy

Pinellas Association of School Administrators is committed to advocating for our members at the district, regional and state level.

MISSION

The Pinellas Association of School Administrators is a professional organization which provides service, support and advocacy for Pinellas County School Board administrators through collaborative practices. We are a group comprised of Principals, Assistant Principals, Superintendents, Assistant Superintendents, Directors, Supervisors and other various administrative positions within the Pinellas County School District. The organization's focus is keeping its members informed of issues that directly affect their membership.

Bylaws

Pinellas Association of School Administrators follow a set of defined bylaws to help the organization and its members control the internal affairs of its members. Bylaws govern the way the group must function as well as the roles and responsibilities of its officers. They are essential in helping our organization map out its purpose and the practical day-to-day details of how we go about our business.
